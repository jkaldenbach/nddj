About

I have a Masters in Business degree, and have owned my 5 star DJ business for 5+ years now with a collection of over 300,000 songs. What sets me apart from other DJ companies is I run a one man operation. The benefit in this is you will have one contact point who singlehandedly helps you plan your special event, helps you select music, and will be the one entertaining at your event. This type of personalization isn’t available at all the other huge DJ companies. I specialize in Weddings, Parties, Karaoke, Dances, or Anything that needs entertainment and music! I am hired often because of my presence on the microphone, and my ability to keep the dance floor full all night. I currently rank in the Top 3 DJ’s in my area and I currently live in South Carolina. I am also a master of the microphone and provide professional Master of Coordination services for almost any event you can think of. I am frequently commended for my ability to speak to a crowd, and is a major reason I am rebooked.

Rates

Weddings

$100 – $150 an hour
For any event within a 50 mile radius of Charleston, SC. For any wedding outside of this 50 mile range (Myrtle Beach, Savannah, North Carolina, etc), there is nominal fee to cover the travel expenses. For example: The average wedding reception runs anywhere from 4­7 hours, so that rate could be $400­$700 depending on how long you needed the service.

Karaoke

$75 – $125 an hour
For any Karaoke event 50 mile outside of Charleston, SC, there is a nominal fee to cover the travel expenses. This is a “price range” because the technology needs of this service can vary greatly. For example: A 5 hour Karaoke party at a house in Charleston, SC usually runs $375.

House Parties, Renunions, Dances, etc.

$50 – $100 an hour
For any event outside of this 50 mile range, there is a nominal fee to cover travel expenses. For example: A 4 hour pool party for children in Charleston, SC could run $200. A 4 hour school dance for hundreds of kids could run $400.
